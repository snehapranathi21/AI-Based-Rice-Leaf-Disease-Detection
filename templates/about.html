WHY IS THE PARTICULAR TOPIC CHOSEN?

• Increased Accuracy: CNNs, especially with transfer learning, have demonstrated superior performance in image classification tasks, making them a promising solution for detecting subtle disease manifestations.

• Efficiency: Automatic classification can significantly reduce the time taken for disease diagnosis compared to manual methods.

• Wider Reach: An automated system can benefit areas with limited access to agricultural experts, ensuring that a larger number of farmers can benefit from early disease detection.

OBJECTIVE OF THE PROJECT

To design, develop, and implement a CNN-based system, enhanced with transfer learning, for the accurate and rapid classification of rice leaf diseases. The goal is to empower farmers and agricultural entities with a reliable tool, enabling them to make informed decisions for timely treatment, ultimately leading to healthier crops and better yields.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT

• Gathering a comprehensive dataset of rice leaf images, capturing various stages of different diseases.
• Leveraging the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with Transfer Learning to classify and identify the diseases.
• Validating the model's accuracy and reliability in varied real-world agricultural settings.
• Deploying a user-friendly application that can be utilized by farmers and agricultural specialists to quickly assess rice leaf health.
